The next month...
My plans for almost every weekday in May look something like this:

  • Wake up.
  • Travel to client (either training or consulting).
  • Spend day on-site with client.
  • Travel to lodgings (home or hotel)
  • Have light meal.
  • Catch up and execute fire-fighting tasks that have accumulated throughout the day, as we're currently short-staffed.
  • Sleep.

While this has the advantage that I'm deriving income from practically every waking moment, it doesn't provide me with a great deal of time for relaxation. I really like relaxation, it's why I started the whole business in the first place.

The good news is that this is probably going to be the most hectic period for quite some time, so I can look forward to a quieter June/July. In October, I even plan to have a holiday.
