Port Phillip diving photographs
Pictures from our most recent diving trip are now online. Jacinta's done some excellent photography, although our batteries went flat during the second dive.

All the regions photographed are close to the proposed areas to be dredged as part of the channel deepening project. It's unlikely that these beautiful habitats will survive if the project proceeds.
