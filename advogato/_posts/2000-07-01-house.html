Permaculture
The local trainstation had a big impressive permaculture poster up which appears to have been sponsored by the train company. It's good to see things like this going up around the city -- sometimes it seems that any poster advocating sustainability lives inside a University campus, or leads a very brief existence before it's plastered over with concert posters.
