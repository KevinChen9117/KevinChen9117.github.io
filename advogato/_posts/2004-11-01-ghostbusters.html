Ghostbusters
One of the television stations is running a 20 year anniversary special of Ghostbusters (and Ghostbusters II). Boy, I sure feel old now. When I was a child I built my own ghostbusting paraphernalia -- we'd catch ghosts using equipment cobbled together from an old vacuum cleaner, turntable, and pram. An evaporative cooling unit was used for containment.

We must have done a pretty good job, as I haven't heard of any ghosts hanging around my old suburb since that time.
